About The Event

The International Conference on Advanced Research in Engineering 2025 (CARE-2025) scheduled on November 27-28, 2025. It aims to provide a global platform for researchers, engineers, and practitioners to present and discuss the latest advancements and innovations in the field of engineering. The conference seeks to foster collaboration and networking among academia, industry, and government sectors to address contemporary engineering challenges.

F.A.Q

  • What is the International Conference 2025?

    The International Conference 2025 is a global event that brings together experts, scholars, professionals, and enthusiasts from various industries and academic fields to discuss cutting-edge developments, share knowledge, and foster collaborations. The conference will feature keynote speakers, panel discussions, workshops, and networking opportunities.

  • When and where will the event take place?

    The International Conference 2025 will be held from November 27-28 at CGC Jhanjeri, located in Mohali.

  • Is there a participation fee?

    Yes, there is a registration fee to attend the conference. The fee varies depending on your registration type (e.g., student, academic, professional). For details on pricing, visit the registration page on our website.

  • Can I present my research at the conference?

    Yes, we welcome submissions for presentations, papers, and posters.
